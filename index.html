About us

Golden Zipper Industrial Group

Golden Zipper Industrial Co., Ltd brings together SNBET® and EasyFull® to provide integrated trims and packaging solutions for apparel brands worldwide. From zippers to plastic packaging and garment hangers, we focus on quality, safety and long-term partnership.

One group, two specialist brands

SNBET® focuses on industrial and kidswear-safe zippers, backed by an in-house testing laboratory and decades of zipper expertise. EasyFull® specializes in PE bags, biodegradable mailers, bubble wrap and garment hangers, completing a one-stop solution for factories and retailers.

Our teams support OEM and ODM projects from idea to mass production, helping brands standardize trims, reduce supplier complexity and scale across regions.

At a glance

  • 20+ years of zipper and packaging manufacturing experience
  • Dedicated facilities for zippers and plastic packaging
  • Comprehensive physical and chemical testing in-house
  • Export to the US, Europe, Japan, Australia and beyond
  • Support for high-volume, multi-country sourcing programs
Solutions

End-to-end trims & packaging portfolio

We help apparel brands streamline their supplier base by combining zippers, garment packaging and hangers under one industrial group, while meeting strict global standards.

SNBET® • Zippers

Industrial & kidswear zippers

Nylon, vislon and metal zippers engineered for fashion, childrenswear, sportswear, uniforms and sleepwear, with options optimized for sensitive products and safety-critical applications.

  • Product types: nylon, vislon, metal, coil, waterproof, double sliders
  • Daily capacity: 350,000 pcs; yearly capacity: 125 million pcs
  • In-house testing for color fastness, tensile, torsion, flammability and more
EasyFull® • Packaging

PE & biodegradable packaging

Custom PE packaging bags (virgin to 100% recycled), biodegradable bags and e-commerce-ready solutions, designed for both factory packing and direct-to-consumer shipping.

  • Virgin, 50% recycled or 100% recycled PE options
  • PLA, PBAT and starch-based biodegradable materials available
  • Custom size, thickness, color, printing, flap style & hanger holes
EasyFull® • Hangers

Garment hangers for factory & retail

Durable PP, PS and ABS hangers tailored for T-shirts, suits, denim, lingerie, kidswear and more, with options for branded and store-specific programs.

  • Custom logo, shape, size, hooks, clips and finishes
  • Optional anti-slip coating, rotating hooks and shoulder notches
  • Optimized for bulk packing, export cartons and DC operations

Service flow

From first inquiry to on-time delivery

Whether you are sourcing zippers, packaging or hangers, our teams follow a clear, transparent process from technical consultation to shipment.

01
Step one

Inquiry & technical consultation

Share your zipper or packaging requirements—product type, materials, sizes, artwork, testing and certifications. Our specialists review feasibility and suggest the best structures and options.

02
Step two

Design, sampling & quotation

For zippers, our product design team prepares constructions and, when needed, develops new molds. For packaging, we create custom samples within around 3–7 days and issue detailed quotations for approval.

03
Step three

Mass production & quality control

Once samples and pricing are confirmed, mass production begins. All key processes are controlled in-house with continuous testing to ensure consistency across lots and destinations.

04
Step four

Packing, documentation & delivery

Finished goods are packed to export standards with clear labeling. We coordinate shipping to your designated warehouses, distribution centers or partner factories globally.
