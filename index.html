About
About Golden Zipper Industrial Group
We integrate SNBET® and EasyFull® to support global brands with zippers, garment packaging and hangers—focused on quality, testing, compliance and stable supply.

One group, three core categories

  • SNBET® Zippers: nylon, vislon, metal, waterproof, double sliders.
  • EasyFull® Packaging: PE bags (virgin to recycled), biodegradable bags, bubble wrap.
  • Garment Hangers: PP/PS/ABS, custom logo, hooks, clips, finishes.

What clients value

  • High-volume manufacturing capacity & lead-time control
  • In-house testing & standardized quality checks
  • Support for chemical / social compliance requirements
  • Program execution across regions

In-house testing (zippers)
