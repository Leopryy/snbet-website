About
About Golden Zipper Industrial Group
We integrate SNBET® and EasyFull® to serve global apparel brands with zippers, garment packaging, and hangers— designed for stable quality, compliance, and long-term supply.

One group, three core categories

  • SNBET® Zippers: nylon, vislon, metal, waterproof, double sliders.
  • EasyFull® Packaging: PE bags (virgin to recycled), biodegradable bags, bubble wrap.
  • Garment Hangers: PP/PS/ABS, custom logo, hooks, clips, finishes.

Why clients work with us

  • High-volume manufacturing capacity
  • In-house testing & quality assurance
  • Support for compliance requirements
  • Program execution across regions
